Reflection

Everyone worked well when coming up with design ideas for the proposed application. Most of our design ideas generated were of high standard, thus we encountered great difficulty in selecting the distinct ones from the variation of ideas developed. This led to positive expectations for the succeeding activities that were performed.

One area of improvement is regarding the payment options by allowing the customers and businesses to specify their option of payment at the registration screen. Also, to make this available in the navigation menu as it will be easier to find and accessible at all times.

We would say that we could have done the sketches via draw.io so that everyone can be able to edit and make changes instead of having to redraw or erase specific designs. By doing this, all team members would have the opportunity to access the sketches and improve or give feedback.
